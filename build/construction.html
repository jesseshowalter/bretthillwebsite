Construction

Brett Hill Construction has built a reputation as a trusted general contractor known for ethics and excellence. A uniquely personal approach involves embracing our clients’ vision and fighting for their interests. We specialize in the following four sectors:

Residential

We offer services for a wide variety of residential construction and remodeling projects, ranging from the build-out of entire condominium projects to individual condo units, custom single-family homes, kitchen and bath remodeling, general repairs and maintenance.
