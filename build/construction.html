Construction

Brett Hill Construction has built a reputation as a trusted general contractor known for ethics and excellence. A uniquely personal approach involves embracing our clients’ vision and fighting for their interests. We specialize in the following four sectors:

Residential

We offer services for a wide variety of residential construction and remodeling projects, ranging from the build-out of entire condominium projects to individual condo units, custom single-family homes, kitchen and bath remodeling, general repairs and maintenance.

The professional workmanship and attitude of the pros from Brett Hill — during all aspects of the renovation project — were outstanding.
- Larry and Patricia Rodriquez, homeowners

Commercial

We have extensive experience in the construction of commercial spaces, ranging from shell+core buildings to individual offices for medical groups, law practices, and professional service firms.

I would recommend Brett Hill Construction, Inc. without reservation. They are professional, courteous and highly skilled.
- Arnold McCaleb, Architect and Space Planner, Pacific Guardian Center

We will use the Brett Hill team again. They were excellent, and the project manager and superintendent were wonderful.
- Eileen Reinholz, Project Manager, Bank of Hawaii

Retail & Resturant

Our retail projects range from turnkey demolition and interior build-outs to remodels and brand new stores and restaurants. We’re accustomed to intricate design with detailing that features woods, metals, and glasswork.

Brett Hill Construction was instrumental in planning and building various components for Turtle Bay Resort. We’ve developed a high level of trust with them, which has proven to bring our projects in as budgeted and on schedule.
- Scott McCormack, Vice President, Replay Resorts LLC

Health & Education

Our knowledge and experience in educational and medical technology enable us to work shoulder-to-shoulder with our clients and their specialty architects, achieving a project’s goals in a timely, efficient, and cost-sensitive manner.
